today we'll study about the lahori fish fry recipe.

ingrredients:

  1. besan1 cup
  2. red mirch 1 tbsp
  3. yoghurt 2 tbsp
  4. salt as taste
  5. 2 lemon syrup
  6. ajwaien 1 tbsp
  7. chaat masala 11/2
  8. red food color
  9. water 1 cup
  10. oil for fry
  11. fish 1 kg

recipe:

  • pour all ingredient except fish in a bowl and made a paste through using water, then marinate fish with this mixtue. and leave it for 2 hours.
  • pour oil in the pan and shallow fry the marinate pieces of fish.
  • fry the fish unless its color is turned to golden.
  • serve in plate, enjoy!
